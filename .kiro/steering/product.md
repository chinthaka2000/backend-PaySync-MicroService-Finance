# PaySync - Microfinance Application

PaySync is a finance company application designed to help people purchase products through loan services. The system manages loan applications, client onboarding, staff roles, and payment processing.

## Core Features
- Client registration and loan application processing
- Multi-tier staff management (CEO, Regional Managers, Agents, Moderate Admins)
- Document verification and approval workflows
- Regional-based loan management across Sri Lankan districts
- Payment tracking and installment management

## Business Model
The application serves as a microfinance platform where clients can apply for loans to purchase products, with a structured approval process managed by different staff roles across various regions in Sri Lanka.
